Who Is Ben?

I'm a software developer with experience in JavaScript, React, Redux, Ruby and Ruby on Rails. Shortly after graduating from App Academy, an intensive 1000 hour coding course, I joined App Academy's staff as a Hacker in Residence. As a Hacker in Residence, I give lectures on programming fundamentals, including data structures and algorithms, and give individual instruction to prospective students. I also conduct technical interviews of applicants in JavaScript and Ruby.

I received my Bachelor's degree from The Juilliard School and spent 2 years performing in the State of Mexico Symphony Orchestra (OSEM), in Toluca, Mexico. I can be heard on the Grammy winning CD, "Centennial: Newly Discovered Works of Gil Evans" and have worked in business immigration at Goldman Sachs and Fragomen, Del Rey, Bernsen & Loewy, LLP.
