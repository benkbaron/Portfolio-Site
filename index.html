As Told By Ben

I'm a software developer with experience in JavaScript, React, Redux, Ruby and Ruby on Rails. Shortly after graduating from App Academy, an intensive 1000 hour coding course, I joined App Academy's staff as a Hacker in Residence. As a Hacker in Residence, I prepare prospective students for App Academy's rigorous course through individual instruction and by giving lectures on programming fundamentals, including data structures and algorithms. I also conduct technical and non-technical interviews of prospective students. I love creating web apps with a smooth and intuitive user experience and squashing bugs like there's no tomorrow.


Prior to becoming a developer, I worked in business immigration, helping technology teams file H-1B visa and green card applications. I began as a paralegal at Fragomen, Del Rey, Bernsen & Loewy, LLP and later worked as an Immigration Coordinator on-site at Goldman Sachs.


I received a Bachelor of Music degree from The Juilliard School and spent 2 years performing in the State of Mexico Symphony Orchestra (OSEM), in Toluca, Mexico. I've collaborated with international soloists and can be heard on the Grammy winning CD, "Centennial: Newly Discovered Works of Gil Evans."
