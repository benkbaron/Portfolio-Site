About Ben

I'm a software developer with experience in JavaScript, React/Redux, Ruby and Ruby on Rails. As a graduate of App Academy, an intensive 1000 hour coding course, I enjoy creating web apps with a smooth and intuitive user experience and squashing bugs like there's no tomorrow.


Prior to becoming a developer I worked in business immigration, both on-site at a major financial institution and as a paralegal at Fragomen, Del Rey, Bernsen & Loewy, LLP.


I received a Bachelor of Music degree from The Juilliard School and spent 2 years performing in the State of Mexico Symphony Orchestra, in Toluca, Mexico. I can be heard on the Grammy winning CD, "Centennial: Newly Discovered Works of Gil Evans."
